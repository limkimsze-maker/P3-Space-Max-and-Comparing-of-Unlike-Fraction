Space Max and Comparing Unlike Fractions

Help Compute arm the defence rocket by solving Comparing Unlike Fractions questions before the asteroid arrives.

Space Max walks across a strange planet and sees Compute, an anxious alien helper watching the sky.

Compute explains that asteroids are quite common here, but one is now heading straight for the planet.

To arm the defence rocket, Space Max must complete the Comparing Unlike Fractions test. Compute promises him the Navigation Chip after the mission.

Compute also learns that Space Max was captured by an alien and is collecting rocket parts to build a rocket back to Earth.

How to play

Move: Use WASD / arrow keys on laptop, or the on-screen arrow pads on mobile.

Mission rule: Practice is optional preparation. Test Mission must be 12/12 in one sitting to arm the defence rocket and collect the Navigation Chip.

Unlike fraction hint: Make the denominators the same, or compare the fraction values carefully before choosing the greater fraction.
